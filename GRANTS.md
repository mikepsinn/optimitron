## A. ZK Privacy Grants

### 1. Aztec Network Grants

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Program** | [Aztec Grants Program](https://aztec.network/blog/introducing-aztec-grants-funding-a-community-led-privacy-ecosystem) |
| **Grant Size** | $5,000 – $150,000 (RFP-specific grants up to $150K; general grants typically $5K–$50K) |
| **Deadline** | Rolling (grant waves announced periodically; Wave 3+ ongoing) |
| **What They Fund** | Applications built on Aztec's privacy-preserving L2 using the Noir ZK language; cross-chain bridges, privacy tooling, private voting, DeFi privacy |
| **Status** | 🟢 Open — Aztec mainnet (Ignition Chain) launched Nov 2025; ecosystem grants actively funding 50+ contributors |
| **Our Pitch** | Optimitron's Digital Twin Safe needs private health data proofs — users prove aggregate health outcomes without revealing raw data. Aztec's Noir language + programmable privacy L2 is ideal for building ZK circuits that verify causal inference results (effect sizes) while keeping individual time-series data encrypted on-device. |
