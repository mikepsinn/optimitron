# Funding Plan: Optimocracy US Federal Pilot

## Goal
Ship a public, reproducible US federal policy + budget report that demonstrates an evidence-based advisory layer for governance.

## Why Funders, Not Government
The system is designed to counter captured allocation. Initial funding must be external, mission-aligned, and nonpartisan.

## Pilot Deliverables (12 Months)
1. Monthly US federal policy and budget reports generated by a reproducible pipeline.
2. A verification layer that cross-checks at least three independent data sources per outcome.
3. Public datasets and methodology notes suitable for external audit.
4. One or more pilot partnerships with civic or policy organizations.

## Funding Target
$350k–$600k to fund a 12-month pilot team and verification layer build-out.

## Use of Funds
1. Data engineering and ingestion hardening.
2. Evidence validation and methodology review.
3. Verification layer implementation and audit tooling.
4. Report production and public dissemination.
5. Legal and operational setup for a nonpartisan research entity.

## Structure (Not Legal Advice)
1. A research entity that publishes the scorecard and methodology.
2. Optional advocacy or independent expenditure entities are separate and counsel-led.

## Run the Funding Pack
1. `pnpm --filter @optomitron/examples generate:optimocracy`
2. Outputs:
3. `packages/examples/output/us-federal-optimocracy-report.md`
4. `packages/examples/output/us-federal-optimocracy-summary.json`
5. `reports/us-federal-optimocracy-report.md`
6. `reports/us-federal-optimocracy-summary.json`
