## Recommendation

**Adopt the approach of Czech Republic, Portugal, Israel.** These countries achieve Infant Survival (inverted mortality) of 97.6 while spending $2898/cap vs the US at $10333/cap for Infant Survival (inverted mortality) 94.83.

Reducing to the efficient floor of $2911/cap would save **$2.5T/yr**, equivalent to **$9,753/yr ($813/mo)** per adult as a Universal Dividend.
