# Universal Dividend Calculator

If the US matched the spending efficiency of the best OECD countries, the savings could fund a Universal Dividend for every adult citizen.

> **Note:** Healthcare savings are counted once (Medicare, Medicaid, and other health programs share the same OECD health spending comparison).

## Citizen Choice Provision

Don't like a reform? Redirect your dividend back to any program you choose. Every citizen can allocate any portion of their payment to any federal budget category. If enough people redirect their savings to defense, it effectively continues at the current level — through voluntary funding, not compulsion.
