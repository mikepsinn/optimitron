# Optomitron / Optimocracy — One-Pager

**What it is**  
A public‑good optimization engine that analyzes historical policy and budget data to publish evidence‑based recommendations that improve health and wealth, then scores politicians on alignment.

**Why now**  
Outcomes are measurable, data is abundant, and the cost of misinformation is rising. We can quantify what works and publish it at scale.

**What’s unique**  
- Domain‑agnostic causal inference engine with transparent scoring.
- Multi‑source verification layer to reduce capture risk.
- Nonpartisan, advisory output with public auditability.

**Pilot (US Federal)**  
Monthly budget + policy reports with top recommendations, evidence grades, and alignment scoring — generated by a reproducible pipeline.

**Ask**  
$350k–$600k to run a 12‑month pilot, harden the verification layer, and publish a public scorecard.

**Outputs**  
Reproducible reports, open methodology, and public datasets for audit and reuse.
