# Protocol Labs Hackathon — Narrative Brief

> Source: user direction from 2026-03-29 session

## Core Narrative Flow

### The Problem
Misaligned superintelligence of government making everyone dead and poor.
Spending 604X more on bombs than clinical trials.

### The Solution
The Earth Optimization Game:
1. Everyone invests 1% of their savings on average into the Earth Optimization Fund
2. Gets all their friends (target: 4B — all voters) to wishocratically allocate how much should be spent on explosions vs clinical trials
3. Figure out how to get their governments to actually do it

### The Incentive
If they succeed and income/lifespan metrics are hit:
- The prize is divided based on how many votes they got
- The fund is now 11X larger than original contributions because it was diversified across VC producing 17%/year

### The Tools (to help them win)
- **Wishocracy** — pairwise preference allocation (how citizens decide budget priorities)
- **Optimal Budget Generator** — find cheapest high-performer for each spending category
- **Optimal Policy Generator** — grade every policy A-F by what actually happened
- **Decentralized FDA** — Outcome Labels + Treatment Rankings (44x cheaper, 12.3x capacity, zero queue)
- **Incentive Alignment Bonds** — incentivize politician alignment with citizen preferences

### Protocol Labs Tech (integrated into features)
- **Hypercerts** — on-chain impact receipts (voter recruitment, deposits, preference comparisons)
- **Storacha + IPFS** — immutable encrypted storage for citizen data (priorities, health outcomes, policy grades)
- **World ID (Worldcoin)** — personhood verification for voters, UBI distribution
